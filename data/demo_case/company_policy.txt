Cedar & Oat Foods Product Safety Escalation Policy

A case must be escalated to the Recall Manager if any of the following occur:

1. More than 5 complaints are linked to the same batch within 7 days.
2. Any complaint mentions urgent care, doctor visit, hospitalization, difficulty breathing, or swelling.
3. Any undeclared major allergen is suspected.
4. A retailer reports label inconsistency.
5. More than 1,000 units remain in market or inventory.

AI agents may recommend actions, but only a human Recall Manager can approve:

- Retailer hold notice.
- Public recall statement.
- Regulator notification.
- Customer-wide notification.

Agents must not tell customers a product is safe if a serious safety signal is under review.
